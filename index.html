GEMINI Dataset Explorer

The GEMINI dataset covers two locations (Davis and Kearney) and spans two years (2022 and 2023). It consists of various data types categorized under Input and Ground Truth.

Definitions of Key Traits:

  • Stand Count: The number of viable plants per plot. A plant is considered viable if it is green, not wilted, and does not exhibit significant yellowing of leaves or stems.
  • Flowering Time:
    • In grain legumes: The date when 50% of the plants in the plot have at least one open flower.
    • In sorghum: The date when 50% of the plants in the plot have anthers extruding pollen on the top 50% of their primary panicle.
  • Plant Height: The distance from the base of the plant to its highest point. The reported value is the estimated average plant height per plot.
  • Yield (kg/ha): The weight of seeds/grains per plot area, adjusted to account for variation in moisture content.
  • Flowering Scoring: A shorthand term for flowering time, referring to the process of recording when flowering occurs.

Input Data includes:

  • Drone Imaging
  • Rover Imaging (T4)
  • Physio Measurement
  • LiDAR Observation

Abbreviations:

  • CP: Cowpea
  • CB: Common Bean
